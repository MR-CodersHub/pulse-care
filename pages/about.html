Mobile Flu Shot & Wellness Clinic Services

Bringing certified preventative healthcare, vaccinations, and wellness programs directly to families, seniors, and workplaces.

Why Trust PulseCare for At-Home Clinical Care?

PulseCare was established with a single, crucial directive: to deliver on-site vaccination clinics and preventative wellness services directly to patients, eliminating the scheduling, travel, and wait-time barriers of traditional offices. We bring high-quality clinical standards directly to your living room or workspace.

Uncompromising Clinical Integrity: All immunizations and therapies are administered exclusively by board-certified Registered Nurses. Our mobile nurse vehicles are equipped with medical-grade refrigeration units that continuously data-log temperature logs, ensuring the 2°C to 8°C cold chain is strictly maintained from depot to administration.

Patient-First Security: We handle pre-screenings and consent records via our secure, fully HIPAA-compliant digital dashboard, keeping patient medical records protected and encrypted. We operate with full OSHA and clinical waste compliance at every visit.

Our Clinical Mission

To maximize seasonal immunity and community wellness defense through board-certified vaccinations and mobile clinics for children, adults, and seniors.

Our Strategic Vision

To expand our active family, senior-living, and B2B corporate mobile clinics nationwide, establishing the highest standard in direct-to-door healthcare delivery.

Patient-First Care Programs

Children & Family Care

Our Registered Nurses are specifically trained in pediatric-friendly administration. We offer stress-free vaccination sessions in the comfort of your child's home, eliminating the clinical anxiety, tears, and waiting rooms associated with standard offices.

Senior Care Support

Protecting older adults is a core clinical priority. We deliver gentle, in-home vaccinations and wellness drips directly to seniors, providing elder-focused consultations, strict safety pre-screenings, and full coordination with family members or retirement community directors.

Coverage & Regional Service

Insurance Coverage & Claims

Most major health insurance plans cover annual flu vaccinations at 100% under preventive care rules. Following your visit, PulseCare provides a detailed, clinical superbill containing standard ICD-10 diagnostic codes for direct insurance reimbursement. We accept out-of-network and direct employer billing options.

Service Areas & Availability

Our active mobile units serve metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs. Travel fees are completely waived for all scheduled group visits (5+ people), and home visits are coordinated to maximize regional routes, ensuring timely nurse arrival and cold-chain clinical integrity.

Clinical Standards

Healthcare Compliance & Care Quality Standards

PulseCare operates under strict regulatory guidelines to ensure every mobile health visit is delivered safely, legally, and professionally.

State Nursing Board Credentials

Every PulseCare practitioner is an active, licensed Registered Nurse (RN) whose credentials and clinical standing are verified directly through State Boards of Nursing. All nurses undergo criminal background checks and mandatory emergency cardiac care training.

OSHA Biohazard Compliance

We maintain strict conformity with OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) regulations. All sharps, syringes, and clinical waste materials are safely contained in certified puncture-resistant receptacles and processed via authorized medical waste services.

CDC Temperature Dataloggers

To guarantee vaccine potency, our portable coolers utilize CDC-recommended digital data loggers (DDLs). These loggers record internal temperatures every 30 seconds, providing verifiable documentation of uninterrupted cold-chain integrity.
