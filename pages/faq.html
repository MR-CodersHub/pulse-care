Safety & Medical Protocols

Is the mobile flu shot safe?

Yes, absolutely. Our vaccines are FDA-approved and held under active cold-chain tracking. All clinics are administered by certified and licensed Registered Nurses (RNs).

What is the quadrivalent vaccine?

It is a clinical immunization standard engineered to protect against four distinct influenza virus strains: two influenza A strains and two influenza B strains, maximizing seasonal immunity.

Booking, Travel, & Scheduling

What is the minimum group size for booking a mobile clinic?

We cater to individuals (home visits), families, and large-scale corporate teams. There is no minimum group size, though group volume discounts apply starting at 5+ individuals.

Insurance & Billing

Will insurance reimburse my vaccine cost?

Most healthcare insurance covers preventative immunizations 100%. We provide detailed clinical super-bill records containing standard diagnostic codes for quick insurance claim submissions.
