Preventative Immunizations

Mobile Flu Shot Clinics for Families & Workplaces

PulseCare brings professional, cold-chain monitored annual flu vaccinations directly to your physical doorstep. Administered by licensed, board-certified Registered Nurses, our mobile setup completely bypasses public clinic waiting rooms and waiting lines.

What is this service?

On-site administration of FDA-approved, preservative-free quadrivalent seasonal influenza vaccines. Our Registered Nurses manage the entire clinical process from sterile draw to patient injection.

Who is it for?

Designed for children (aged 4+ years), adults, seniors requiring high-dose formulations (65+), families, and corporate workplace teams looking for on-site convenience.

Why is it beneficial?

Bypasses waiting rooms to eliminate secondary virus exposure, saves travel time, prevents seasonal influenza with 40-60% documented CDC efficacy, and supports community herd immunity.

How does it work?

Our nurse transports active vaccines in continuous data-logged cold-chain containers (2°C to 8°C). Upon arrival, the nurse reviews medical pre-screening details, checks vitals, administers the dose, and monitors for safety.

How can I access it?

Check your zip code, complete the secure online intake form, and reserve your nurse home visit or corporate team clinic in under two minutes.

Wellness & Hydration

In-Home Wellness Assessments & Nutrient Drips

Maintain high physiological defense and metabolic function without visiting clinics. Our wellness visits incorporate vital health screenings, high-absorption vitamin booster injections, and isotonic IV hydration therapies.

What is this service?

A comprehensive on-site wellness assessment that combines basic vital signs tracking with intramuscular (IM) Vitamin B12 booster injections or customized isotonic IV hydration therapies.

Who is it for?

Vitals screening and B12 boosters are available for all age categories. IV hydration therapies are clinically restricted to adults aged 18 to 65 with normal cardiovascular and renal parameters.

Why is it beneficial?

Bypasses gastrointestinal absorption barriers to deliver 100% bioavailability, restores systemic fluid volume, replenishes trace minerals immediately, and screens vital markers inside a comfortable setting.

How does it work?

A Registered Nurse screens blood pressure, resting heart rate, and blood oxygen levels first. If metrics meet safe boundaries, the nurse administers the B12 deltoid shot or sets up the sterile IV drip, monitoring the client throughout.

How can I access it?

Select the Wellness visit package on our online portal, fill out the clinical background details, and choose an available routing window.
