Terms & Conditions

Please review our standard patient agreement guidelines and operational terms.

1. Patient Screening Declarations

Every scheduled wellness clinical treatment requires a completed patient health consent form prior to nursing administration. Any past allergy alerts, medicine schedules, or vaccine side-effects must be disclosed strictly in the screening form.

2. Booking Cancellations & Travel Fees

Appointments cancelled within 2 hours of nurse deployment may incur a standard $25 travel fee. PulseCare commits to arrival windows of 30 minutes, keeping operations reliable and smooth.

3. Insurance Reimbursement Disclaimers

We accept major insurance plans and supply diagnostic codes superbly. However, absolute preventative wellness reimbursement depends strictly on individual patient network policies.
